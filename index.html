Products

GI wires, or galvanized iron wires, are steel wires coated with zinc for enhanced corrosion resistance and durability, making them suitable for various applications like construction, agriculture, and fencing.

GI (Galvanized Iron) barbed wire is a type of steel fencing wire with sharp edges or points arranged at intervals along the strands, coated with zinc for rust resistance and used for security and property protection.

PVC (polyvinyl chloride) coated wires, commonly used for electrical applications, feature a protective layer of PVC around the wire, enhancing durability, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors like moisture, chemicals, and abrasion.

MS (Mild Steel) binding wire, used in construction, is an annealed steel wire, typically 0.61 mm to 1.22 mm in diameter, used to tie rebar at joints, ensuring structural integrity and holding bars in place during concrete pouring.

MS (Mild Steel) wire nails are common fasteners made from mild steel wire, often galvanized for corrosion resistance, used in construction, carpentry, and various industrial applications to join materials.

Clutch wire, also known as "jhatka wire" or "zatka wire", is a type of galvanized fencing wire, commonly used for electric fencing, particularly with "zatka machines" or solar fence energizers, and is known for its high conductivity and durability.
